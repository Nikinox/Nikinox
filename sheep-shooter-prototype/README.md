#Sheep shooter game

<img width="487" height="366" alt="immagine" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b6464ed8-d49c-4086-b5bf-b2dcd7c35d1c" />


This game was born with this idea: making a game were you killed sheeps and you could upgrade your paper gun and kill more sheeps with a sort of levels: 60 second rounds and 30 seconds of break to upgrade the gun or planning the next round or just taking a "mini-break".

The game ended up being just an incomplete and full of bugs prototype.

If you want to try it, the link is here:
